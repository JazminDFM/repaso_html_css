NewJeans

The group that changed K-POP

Journey of NewJeans' achievements since their debut:

  • Successful debut: Their debut EP, New Jeans, sold over 1 million copies, setting a record for a debut album since 2011.
  • "Ditto" success: The single "Ditto" topped the Circle Digital Chart for 13 consecutive weeks and won Song of the Year at the 2023 MAMA Awards.
  • Award recognition: At the 2023 MAMA Awards, NewJeans won three awards, including 'Song of the Year,' 'Best Female Group,' and 'Best Dance Performance for a Female Group.'
  • EP "OMG": Released in January 2023, it sold 700,000 copies in its first week and surpassed 1 million in total sales.
  • "OMG" on Billboard Hot 100: The single reached No. 74 on the Billboard Hot 100, showcasing their global popularity.
  • First fan meeting: In July 2023, they held their first fan meeting, "Bunnies Camp," in Seoul, with tickets sold out.
  • EP "Get Up": Released in July 2023, it debuted at No. 1 on the Billboard 200, making them the second K-pop girl group to achieve this.
  • Three singles on Billboard Hot 100: With "Super Shy," "ETA," and "Cool with You," they became the first K-pop girl group to have three simultaneous entries on the Billboard Hot 100.
  • Performance at Lollapalooza: In August 2023, they became the first K-pop girl group to perform at Lollapalooza in the United States.
  • Summer Sonic Festival appearance: That same month, they performed at the Summer Sonic Festival in Japan, expanding their international presence.
